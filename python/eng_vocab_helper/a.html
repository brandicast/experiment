國中英語基測出現過 zoo 的考題。

Jeffrey: Excuse me, are you ready to order?

Mr. Wu: Yes, but... Well, what's "ohenstri"?

Jeffrey: Oh, it's a bird from Botaland. It tastes like... chicken! You can try it. It's very delicious.

Mr. Wu: But I'm afraid of new things. I think I'll just have some chicken soup.

Jeffrey: That's a great idea, sir, but the soup tastes just like ohenstri!

In a hospital.
In a zoo.
